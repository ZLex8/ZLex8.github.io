Enlightenment

  • Don't be a stranger to yourself. Know your consciousness.
    Because the more you avoid your inner world, the more disconnected you become from your truth. Knowing your consciousness is the first step toward living with clarity, purpose, and alignment.

  • Life becomes beautiful once you get out of your own way.
    When you step aside and allow your true self to lead, life flows with more ease, beauty, and meaning.

  • You are not your thoughts.
    The awareness that notices them is constant. That awareness is you.

  • You can't control many situations. But you can control how you experience them.
    You can't change what happens, but you can change what it means to you.

  • Don't resist discomfort. What you resist will persist.
    Resistance gives it power.

  • The mind cannot be forced to stay quiet.
    It can only be detached from enough times to the point where it stops using its energy to get a reaction out of you

  • The living and enjoying your life part is just as important as the lesson learning part.
    Living fully means allowing space for both the lessons and the experiences that make life real.
