Understand the Impermanence of Who You Are Now

You are not meant to stay the same. Who you are today is only a temporary expression, a rhythm shaped by past experiences, belief systems, and cycles. Andromeda invites you to observe these patterns not with judgement, but with consciousness. When you understand the impermanence of the current self, you spread your wings to growth, release, and transformation. You are not broken. You are becoming.
