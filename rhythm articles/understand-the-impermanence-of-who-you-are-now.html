Understanding the Impermanence of Who You Are Now

So many live trapped in the illusion that they must define themselves with permanence. But what if identity was never meant to be fixed? What if change is not a loss of self, but a return to your fluid nature?

This is a call for you to observe yourself with awareness, not attachment. Every reaction, every thought loop, every habit is part of a greater rhythm that can be seen, felt, and shifted. The key is not to judge, but to become conscious of the pattern.
